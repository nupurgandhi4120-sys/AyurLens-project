# AyurLens: Medicinal Plant Companion

AyurLens is an AI-powered platform developed for the Bharat Academix CodeQuest Hackathon. Its mission is to bridge India’s rich AYUSH heritage with modern AI technology, making traditional botanical knowledge accessible, safe, and interactive for everyone.

---

## 💡 Problem Statement
Traditional knowledge regarding medicinal plants is often scattered across multiple sources, leading to several challenges:
* **Identification Gap:** Most people cannot correctly identify medicinal plants.
* **Knowledge Fragmentation:** Information is scattered across multiple sources.
* **Safety Risks:** Misidentification of plants can be risky.
* **Generational Disconnect:** Traditional knowledge is becoming less accessible to younger generations.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Instant AI Identification:** Identify medicinal plants in seconds using advanced image recognition.
* **Verified AYUSH Insights:** Access credible, government-backed information for every plant.
* **Digital Herbal Library:** Browse an extensive database of medicinal uses and precautions.
* **Interactive Learning:** Safely explore botanical knowledge through an AI-powered assistant.

---

## 🛠 Technology Stack
The platform utilizes a modern, scalable tech stack:

* **Frontend:** React 19, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, HTML 5, and Lucide React.
* **AI & API Integration:** Google Gemini 1.5 Flash API, Plant.id, and Wikipedia.
* **Development & Deployment:** Built with Vite and Node.js, and deployed via Netlify.

---

## ⚙️ Core Workflow
The user journey is designed to be seamless and intuitive:

1.  **Open App:** Land on the intuitive, user-friendly homepage.
2.  **Scan the Plant:** Use the camera or upload an image to initiate the identification process.
3.  **Fetch Live Data:** The AI engine processes the image and retrieves relevant data.
4.  **View Results:** Access the plant name and in-depth botanical, safety, and usage details.

---

## 📈 Future Innovations
We are continuously looking to expand the platform's capabilities:
* **Enhanced Profiles:** Adding more comprehensive data on uses, dosages, safety, and cultivation tips.
* **Offline Access:** Enabling library access and saved scan history for offline use.
* **Community Features:** Implementing user contributions, favorites, and shared collections.
* **AR Integration:** Introducing AR overlays for plant comparisons and interactive learning modules.
